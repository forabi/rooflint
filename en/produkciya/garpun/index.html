    Components for ceilings

    Harpoon

    Harpoon - is a special element of soft PVC, which provides tension and fixing web profile. It prevents its sagging with time. Harpoon does not leak, and the film does a ceiling which is resistant to leaks. Fixing harpoon to stretch ceilings is made sol- dered on the perimeter of the entire film.

    PS-105

    We use the best harpoon, which is on the market. This type of harpoon is firmly attached to the profile, does not jump out of it and makes the process of installation so fast and quality of installation so good.

    Profile

    The basis of all great and small structures - a sturdy aluminum profile. On it is kept the world, not just your suspended ceiling. Our company offers you to buy it, aluminum and PVC profile. It is:

    • profile wall PVC, standard length 2.5 meters;

    • aluminum profile wall.

    Their difference is - how to secure the suspended ceiling: the first type is connectioned with the simple ceiling, the second - with the wall.

    You need the next constraction:

    • separating the aluminum profile, slot and gapless;

    • PVC profile separator, slotted and gapless;

    • Baguette PVC wall;

    • constructive profile, slot and gapless;

    And decorative elements for the final decoration:

    • white and colored insert for support section;

    • inserting in a separator.

    We provide not only reasonable prices but also the quality of all components, and the basis for cooperation: the formation of orders so quickly and attention to the terms, and your desires. The best conditions - for our dealers!

    Aluminum spacer

    Lighting

    Installation of a ceiling is always accompanied with the installation of lighting and lighting can be different - like your client wants. And the next:

    • your people need to have access for electrical work;

    • it is important to have skills for work with tension ceilings;

    • richassortement for different customer's wishes

    You can decide first two points by yourself, but Let's look at the last aspect - The lighting has technical requirements, they should be:

    • safe; give enough light; work many years.

    Stretch ceilings have many variants of lighting design. There are:

    • a classic chandelier of Bohemian glass;

    • spotlights (which are for suspended and plasterboard structures);

    • front lights for the multilevel ceilings;

    • the backlight inside

    Designers are inventing new ways to surprise the world and masters of the stretch ceiling need to keep up with fashion trends, and news . Spotlights are designed for LED light bulbs - are so popular now They are economical and provide stunning light under a stretch ceiling.
