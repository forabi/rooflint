    About Us

    Rooflint Company is a manufacturer of suspended ceilings and was founded in 2011. We - the Ukrainian company with 7 years of experience, professionally make the production of suspended ceilings, which are characterized by a high degree of reliability and excellent quality. The main principle of the company's development is to maximize the convenience and comfort of customers (dealers). For the convenience of our dealers, we offer not only the suspended ceilings which are made on customer's drawings, and completely ready to be installed at the site, - the company has a complete range of accessories for the installation of suspended ceilings "Turnkey" with a minimum mark-up. Full range of colours - are always in the company.

    Main goal

    Be a reliable partner for our customers, provide them with quality products.


    Idea

    Produce ceilings, attendant products and services, exceeding the expectations of partners. To make efficient tools for their work, to build the work of our company and that it will be a model of workability.
