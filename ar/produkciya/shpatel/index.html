    The spatula for INSTALLATION of Suspended Ceilings

    Due to numerous requests of our partners, we have developed our own production of spatulas, which are necessary for the installation of suspended ceilings. In their desigh, we focused on easy using in the installation - manufacture tooling (shovel) under a special coner to the installation spatula not rested in the wall; We developed a comfortable convenient handle, which is made from wood; We added special ribs to snap. Today, we manufacture two types of spatulas, wide and narrow (large and small), with which you can close all the issues during installation of the web.

    Spatula narrow

    It is used for mounting in tight spaces. The blade has special ribs and is made of titanium or steel.

    Spatula wide

    It is used for quick mounting on straight surfaces. The blade has special ribs and is made of titanium or steel.
